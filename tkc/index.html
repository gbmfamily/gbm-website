About The Kingdom Camp

TKC is an annual outreach-focused gathering for children and teenagers from different backgrounds and denominations, designed to create a safe, joyful, and spiritually enriching environment grounded in God’s Word.

Spiritual Formation

Through Bible teaching, prayer, worship, and group discussions, campers are helped to understand faith in age-appropriate ways rooted in Scripture.

Family-Style Groups

Campers are placed in mixed “families” led by trained adults to experience mentorship, accountability, teamwork, and belonging.

Engaging Activities

Alongside spiritual sessions, TKC includes games, competitions, creative activities, and shared meals to make learning memorable.

Theme: Faithful & Fruitful

This year’s camp is centered around growing in faith and living fruitfully in Christ—confidently in homes, schools, and communities.

Packing List

Mandatory

  • Bible
  • Notebook / writing materials
  • Personal toiletries
  • Modest clothing for camp duration
  • Nightwear
  • Slippers and covered shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Small backpack or tote

Health & Safety

  • Personal medications (clearly labeled)
  • Mosquito repellent
  • Face towel / handkerchief

Optional (Helpful)

  • Bible storybook (for children)
  • Flashlight / torch
  • Sweater or light jacket (for evenings)

Not Allowed

  • Expensive gadgets
  • Games or materials that distract from camp activities
