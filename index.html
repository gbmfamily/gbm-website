Vision

Bringing many Sons to glory.
Hebrews 2:10

Mission

As a dynamic church proclaiming the Word of God, our Mission is to make disciples for our Lord Jesus Christ by:

  • Exalting God
  • Encouraging believers
  • Equipping Christians for ministry
  • Evangelizing the World

Motto

Preach, teach, disciple. Colossians 1:28; II Timothy 2:2

Statement of Purpose

  • Lead men to be genuinely born again, saved from sin, idolatry, false religion, selfishness, corruption, and all that debar men from heaven through the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • Build men up to spiritual maturity, becoming Christ-like at home, work, society, and church through the fullness and power of the Holy Spirit.
  • Lead men to walk in dominion over every demonic influence and power through the supernatural power of God.
  • Train and equip men to fulfill the Great Commission, evangelizing the world for Christ and receiving eternal reward.
  • Plant Acts-of-the-Apostles churches (Grace Bible Churches) globally, beginning from Osogbo and spreading through Osun State, Nigeria, Africa, and the world.
  • Prepare men without spot or wrinkle for the glorious appearing of our Lord.
  • Glorify God in worship, ministry, and all we do.
  • Do all other things incidental or conducive to these objectives.
