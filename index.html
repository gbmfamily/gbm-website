Welcome

Grace Bible Mission is a Christ-centered family of believers where people from all walks of life are welcomed, loved, and nurtured in the truth of God’s Word. We believe church is not just a place to attend, but a home where lives are transformed and faith is strengthened.

Whether you are seeking answers, looking for a spiritual family, or desiring to grow deeper in your walk with God, you are warmly welcome to worship with us.

Vision

Bringing many sons to glory.

Hebrews 2:10

Mission

As a dynamic church proclaiming the Word of God, our mission is to make disciples for our Lord Jesus Christ by:

  • Exalting God
  • Encouraging believers
  • Equipping Christians for ministry
  • Evangelizing the world

Motto

Preach, teach, disciple.

Colossians 1:28; II Timothy 2:2

Statement of Purpose

  • To lead men to be genuinely born again, saved from sin, bondage to idolatry, false religion, selfishness, corruption, and all that debars men from heaven through the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • To build men up to spiritual maturity and Christ-likeness at home, at work, in society, and in the house of God through the indwelling power of the Holy Spirit.
  • To lead men to walk in dominion over every demonic influence and power through the supernatural power of God, enjoying the full benefits of the gospel.
  • To train and equip men to fulfill the Great Commission of evangelizing the world for Christ and receiving eternal reward.
  • To plant Acts-of-the-Apostles churches (Grace Bible Churches) across the world, beginning from Osogbo and spreading through Osun State, Nigeria, Africa, and the world.
  • To prepare men without spot or wrinkle for the glorious appearing of our Lord Jesus Christ, that they may enjoy the Kingdom of God forever.
  • To glorify God in worship, in ministry, and in all that we do.
  • To do all other things incidental or conducive to the attainment of these objectives.
