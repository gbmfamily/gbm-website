Grace Bible Mission

A family of believers. A home of worship. A place to grow.

Welcome

Grace Bible Mission is a Christ-centered family where everyone is welcomed, loved, and nurtured in the truth of God’s Word. We are a church that believes in community, spiritual growth, and the power of the gospel to transform lives.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a spiritual home, you are warmly invited to join us.

Sunday Service: 9:00 AM (Every Sunday)

Vision

Bringing many sons to glory.

Hebrews 2:10

Mission

As a dynamic church proclaiming the Word of God, our mission is to make disciples for our Lord Jesus Christ by:

  • Exalting God
  • Encouraging believers
  • Equipping Christians for ministry
  • Evangelizing the world

Motto

Preach, teach, disciple.

Colossians 1:28; II Timothy 2:2

Statement of Purpose

  • To lead men to be genuinely born again, saved from sin, bondage to idolatry, false religion, selfishness, corruption and all that debar men from heaven through the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • To build men up to grow up to maturity spiritually and become Christ-like at home, at work, in the society and in the house of God through the fullness and indwelling power of the Holy Spirit.
  • To lead men to walk in dominion over every demonic influence and power through the supernatural power of God, so as to enjoy the full benefits of the gospel.
  • To train and equip men to fulfil the great commission of evangelizing the world for Christ and receive eternal reward.
  • To plant Acts-of-the-Apostles churches i.e. Grace Bible Churches, through the whole world before our Lord returns, beginning from Osogbo and widening therefrom in ripples through Osun State, Nigeria, Africa and the World.
  • To prepare men without spot or wrinkle for the glorious appearing of our Lord from heaven, that they may enjoy the Kingdom of God forever.
  • To glorify God in worship, in ministry and in all we do.
  • To do all such other things that are incidental or conducive to the attainment of the objectives mentioned above.
